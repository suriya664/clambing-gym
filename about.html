About Summit Climb

Building a community of passionate climbers since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Summit Climb began with a simple mission: to make rock climbing accessible, safe, and fun for everyone. What started as a small bouldering gym has grown into one of the region's premier climbing facilities.

Our founders, passionate climbers themselves, recognized the need for a welcoming space where beginners could learn and experienced climbers could push their limits. Today, we serve over 5,000 members and continue to grow our community.

We're committed to fostering a supportive environment where climbers of all ages and abilities can challenge themselves, make friends, and discover the joy of climbing.

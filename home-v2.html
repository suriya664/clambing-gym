Everything You Need to Climb

Premium facilities and expert support for climbers of all levels

Bouldering Area

Our expansive bouldering area features walls up to 15 feet high with premium crash pads throughout. With over 60 problems ranging from V0 to V10, there's something for every skill level.

  • 15-foot walls with varied angles and features
  • Premium crash pads for safe landings
  • Overhangs, slabs, and vertical terrain
  • Color-coded routes for easy identification
  • Dedicated warm-up and beginner zones
Start Bouldering

Top-Rope & Lead Climbing

Experience the thrill of height on our 45-foot climbing walls. Whether you're learning to top-rope or pushing your limits on lead, we have the perfect terrain.

  • 45-foot walls with auto-belays and rope stations
  • 40+ top-rope and lead routes (5.6 to 5.13)
  • Multiple auto-belay stations for solo climbing
  • Lead climbing with quickdraws provided
  • Varied terrain including cracks, aretes, and faces
Start Rope Climbing

Training Zone

Take your climbing to the next level with our dedicated training area equipped with professional-grade equipment for strength and technique development.

  • Campus boards for power development
  • Multiple hangboards for finger strength
  • System wall for technique training
  • Free weights and resistance equipment
  • Yoga mats and stretching area

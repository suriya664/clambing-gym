Assumption of Risk and Waiver of Liability

By entering Summit Climb and participating in climbing activities, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

1. Acknowledgment of Risk

I understand that rock climbing, bouldering, and related activities are inherently dangerous and involve risks of serious injury or death. These risks include, but are not limited to:

  • Falls from climbing walls or bouldering problems
  • Injuries from falling objects or other climbers
  • Equipment failure or misuse
  • Collisions with walls, holds, or other persons
  • Muscle strains, sprains, fractures, and other physical injuries
  • Head injuries, spinal injuries, or paralysis
  • Death

2. Voluntary Participation

I am voluntarily participating in climbing activities at Summit Climb with knowledge of the inherent dangers involved. I expressly assume all risks associated with participation, whether known or unknown.

3. Physical Condition

I certify that I am in good physical condition and have no medical conditions that would prevent me from safely participating in climbing activities. I agree to immediately inform staff of any changes to my physical condition.

4. Release of Liability

In consideration for being permitted to use Summit Climb's facilities and participate in activities, I hereby release, waive, discharge, and covenant not to sue Summit Climb, its owners, employees, instructors, and agents from any and all liability, claims, demands, or causes of action arising from:

  • My participation in climbing activities
  • Use of equipment and facilities
  • Any injury, illness, or death that may result
  • Any loss or damage to personal property

5. Indemnification

I agree to indemnify and hold harmless Summit Climb from any loss, liability, damage, or costs that may arise from my participation in climbing activities or my use of the facilities.

6. Safety Rules and Regulations

I agree to:

  • Follow all posted rules and staff instructions
  • Use equipment properly and as instructed
  • Inspect equipment before use and report any defects
  • Climb within my ability level
  • Be aware of other climbers and maintain a safe environment
  • Not participate while under the influence of drugs or alcohol

7. Medical Treatment

I authorize Summit Climb staff to obtain emergency medical treatment for me if necessary. I agree to be financially responsible for any medical costs incurred.

8. Photo and Video Release

I grant Summit Climb permission to use photographs or videos taken of me during my visit for promotional purposes, including but not limited to social media, website, and marketing materials.

9. Minors

For participants under 18 years of age, a parent or legal guardian must sign this waiver on behalf of the minor. The parent/guardian agrees to all terms and accepts full responsibility for the minor's participation.

10. Severability

If any provision of this waiver is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

11. Governing Law

This waiver shall be governed by the laws of the State of Colorado, and any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Boulder County, Colorado.

Acknowledgment
